Samsung Corporation, a South Korean conglomerate, is a global leader in technology, electronics, and various industries.
Founded in 1938, it has become one of the world's largest and most influential companies, manufacturing a wide range of
products, including smartphones, appliances, semiconductors, and more. Known for innovation and quality, Samsung plays a
significant role in shaping the consumer electronics market. The company also has diverse business interests, including finance,
construction, and healthcare.
